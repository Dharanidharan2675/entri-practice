Madurai Meenakshi temple is awesome

Trisha

The Meenakshi Amman Temple in Madurai, Tamil Nadu, is a colossal 14-acre historic Hindu complex dedicated to Goddess Meenakshi (a form of Parvati) and her consort, Lord Sundareswarar (Shiva). As the cultural heart of Madurai, it is famed for its 14 towering gopurams blanketed in thousands of brightly painted, sculptural figures.
